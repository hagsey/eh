  • About

    • About

    My full-time work is at a private training center in Cambridge, Ontario called NLPT, where I train some of the best young athletes in the province.

    I've been there for 6 years and currently serve as the Director of Training, which is a title I totally made up, but basically means I'm responsible for our training philosophy, getting our message out, and working with the rest of our coaching staff.

    I write my thoughts down at KeepIt137, a site I created to help me stick with the habit of running.

    On the side, I create websites for my friends and enjoy studying design and front-end development.
